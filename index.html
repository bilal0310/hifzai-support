‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start memorizing a surah?

From the home screen, tap "Memorize" and select any surah. The app will guide you through verse-by-verse memorization with audio playback and spaced repetition.

How does the AI Tajweed correction work?

Record your recitation and our AI will analyze your pronunciation, providing feedback on tajweed rules like Idgham, Ikhfa, and Qalqalah.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! Once you've downloaded surah audio, you can practice memorization offline. Your progress syncs when you reconnect.

How do I manage my subscription?

Go to Settings > Subscription to view your plan. To cancel or change your subscription, use your device's App Store settings.

How do I restore my purchases on a new device?

Sign in with the same account, then go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Your Pro subscription will be restored automatically.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. Your memorization progress is stored securely and encrypted. We never share your personal data with third parties.
